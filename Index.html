What the factors mean
Squad Value
How pricey the average player is, per Transfermarkt. A rough stand-in for talent level.
Squad Age
How experienced (or young and fresh) the squad is on average.
Search Buzz
How much more people have been Googling the team lately. Big spikes usually mean a surprise run or a debut, not necessarily a better team.
Weather Shock
How different the team's home climate is from Miami's heat and humidity — the theory being a bigger jump is a tougher adjustment.
Ruler's Scrabble Score
The Scrabble tile value of the country's current leader's name. Just for fun — no real connection to football.
Group Stage Form
Points earned in this World Cup's group stage (out of 9). How well the team has actually been playing here.
